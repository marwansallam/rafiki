Solomon Liou

Chief Executive Officer

Solomon is the Chief Executive Officer at KidPass. Solomon is a serial entrepreneur with a background in product, design, and growth. Prior to KidPass, he founded and sold eLife Technologies, a social networking company, and Parke, an ecommerce website. He also previously worked at Yahoo, Clickable, AHAlife, and Goldman Sachs. Solomon started KidPass with a group of friends who saw a need as parents to create a better way to discover kids activities. He received his BA from Harvard and his MBA from The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
